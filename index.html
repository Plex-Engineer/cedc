What is the CEDC:

The Canto Ecosystem Development Cluster (CEDC) is a newly formed Canto Commons cluster that aims to grow the Canto ecosystem by assisting in business development initiatives.

What we plan to do:

Currently, we plan to do the following:

  • Bring new integrations to Canto by working with established protocols and projects.
  • Assist newly formed teams working on Canto by offering technical and marketing resources to support their development efforts.
  • Collaborate with the Canto community to identify most desired new potential integrations and current infrastructure gaps in the ecosystem.
  • Contact potential new integrations that align with the community's preferences and work to integrate them into the chain.
  • Foster connections between protocols in the Canto ecosystem to enhance their user experience.
  • Develop tools that make business development on Canto more efficient and transparent.

In addition, the CEDC intends to create the Canto Ecosystem Directory website, which will feature the following:
  1. An open forum for community members to participate in and discuss Canto's business development initiatives.
  2. A directory of the Canto ecosystem that showcases all existing protocols and projects.
  3. A submission form for new projects and protocols seeking assistance.

How to get involved:
The CEDC is an inclusive community that welcomes individuals who are passionate about contributing. To get involved in the CEDC, please reach out through Twitter or join our Telegram group. Twitter: @cedc_canto Telegram: @cedctelegram
